Catzy Support

Welcome to the Catzy support page. Here you'll find answers to common questions and ways to get in touch with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is Catzy?

Catzy is a cat breed identification and AI image feature app designed to help cat lovers understand their feline friends better.

2. Does Catzy store my photos?

Photos used for breed identification and AI features are processed in real-time and not stored. However, if you share images to the Catzy Community, those are stored for display.

3. How do I delete a shared image from the Community?

You can request removal by contacting us via email with the details of the image you shared.

4. What should I do if the breed identification seems incorrect?

Our AI is constantly improving, but it may occasionally make mistakes. Feel free to try another image or contact us with feedback.
